At Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Group, we have consistently pioneered innovative manufacturing technologies to make a lasting difference in the communities we serve. Starting with ship building, we now provide solutions to a broad range of industries from railways and other transportation systems to the energy sector, and space development. Building on our know-how and track record of over 130 years, we continue our quest to advance the lives of people around the world. Learn more about some of our achievements highlighted in our case studies, below.
